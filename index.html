About Me

I am currently in my second year studying BSc (Hons) Cyber Security at RGU, Aberdeen. This follows on from studying HND Computing Science at UHI Inverness which allowed me to experience a wide range of areas of computing, and Web Design Fundamentals on day release at UHI Moray during S6 at High School. From my first year here at RGU and the HND, I now have a broad range of knowledge and it has helped me to discover the areas that interest me and establish my strengths.

I have particular interest in security and design and enjoy the challenge of design and development. From the range of topics studied, I find my strengths lie in forensics, databases and web development. The course has allowed me to develop skills in self-motivation/self-study, team working and project management. It has also helped extend my IT knowledge in both software and hardware and recognise the importance of timescales, meeting deadlines and priorities.

I am creative and enjoy designing and drawing and can come up with individual concepts.
